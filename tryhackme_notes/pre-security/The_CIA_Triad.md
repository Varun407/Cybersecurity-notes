# Introduction to Cyber Security - CIA Triad Revision Notes

## What I Learned

Cyber security is focused on protecting digital data, systems, and services from unauthorized access, modification, and disruption.

The foundation of cyber security is the **CIA Triad**, which consists of:

- Confidentiality
- Integrity
- Availability

These three principles define what cyber security aims to protect.

---

## CIA Triad

### Confidentiality

Confidentiality ensures that data is only accessible to authorized individuals.

### Examples

✅ Employees can access only the documents required for their work.

❌ Personal documents are publicly available online.

❌ Passwords are written on sticky notes and left visible.

### Common Protection Methods

- Encryption
- Access Control
- Authentication

---

### Integrity

Integrity ensures that data cannot be modified without authorization.

Data should remain accurate, complete, and trustworthy.

### Examples

✅ Data updated through an approved process.

❌ Student attendance records changed after being finalized.

❌ Order prices modified before checkout.

### Key Point

If data becomes untrustworthy or altered without permission, integrity has been compromised.

---

### Availability

Availability ensures that data, systems, and services are accessible when needed.

### Examples

✅ Employees can access systems during working hours.

❌ Company website becomes unavailable during business hours.

❌ Critical services stop working after software installation.

### Common Protection Methods

- Backups
- Redundancy
- Load Balancing
- Disaster Recovery Plans

---

## How Security Professionals Use the CIA Triad

When a security incident occurs, analysts often ask:

- Was sensitive information exposed?
  - → Confidentiality

- Was data changed without authorization?
  - → Integrity

- Were systems or services unavailable?
  - → Availability

Understanding which pillar is affected helps determine the impact and response.

---

## Quick Revision

- Cyber security protects data, systems, and services.
- CIA Triad = Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability.
- Confidentiality → Prevent unauthorized access.
- Integrity → Prevent unauthorized modification.
- Availability → Ensure access when needed.
- Most security incidents can be analyzed using the CIA Triad.
- CIA Triad is one of the most important foundational concepts in cyber security.
